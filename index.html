Healthcare Strategy LAB

Health Stratergy Laboratory

Health strategy Lab develops innovative strategies and provides critical analyses necessary to provide policy initiatives progression in public health domain. The main research topics focus on medical industry, medical policy and medical big data.

Our Research Streams

Healthcare Markets

- Social Network Analysis(SNA) of Healthcare Industry

- Ecosystem Analysis of Healthcare Industry

Healthcare Regulations

- Healthcare Policy Network Analysis

- Regulation Analysis In Healthcare Industry

Big Data in Healthcare

- Data Mining of Healthcare System

- Panel Data based analysis of Healthcare System

Postdoctoral Position

We are currently seeking a highly motivated postdoctoral research scientist to work on (1) Healthcare Industry, (2) Healthcare Regulations, (3) Big Data Analysis. If you have a strong background in at least two of the fields mentioned above, please send a merged PDF of your CV, contact information of two references and a brief statement of research interests, and PDFs of your three best papers to and please note '[SAIHST postdoc]' in the subject line.

Graduate Students Position

If you are interested in PhD or MS-PhD courses in our lab, please contact us 6 months before application by sending a merged PDF of your cover letter, CV and academic record (transcript) of your undergraduate to Please note '[SAIHST graduate]' in the subject line.
